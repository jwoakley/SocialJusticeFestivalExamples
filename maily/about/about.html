Why Mental Health Matters


Speak Your Mind is dedicated to providing an unforgettable experience where people leave with new perspectives on mental health. We exist to foster a collective that invites you to Speak Your Mind and help destigmatize mental health.

What Does "Destigmatization" Mean?

It can feel like a big word...

Destigmatization means to remove the association of shame. Talking about mental health is often hard because people carry a sense of shame or judgment around the issue. This is because our society has created an environment where people think that mental health issues are a bad thing, that they hinder a person, that emotions should be kept in the dark, or people do not want to hear about your “problems.” But we are in an age where 1 in 4 people suffer from at least one mental health issue and it is time to break the shame around speaking about it. Too many lives have been lost, too many battles faced alone, too many people suffering in silence. Destigmatization is a step towards fostering an environment where people can speak openly about their mental health, communicate their needs, receive support, and reach resources.

Our Partnership with the Mental Health Coalition

We are Proud to Support the Mental Health Coalition

The Mental Health Coalition is a non-profit organization founded in 2020 amid the peak of the pandemic. They strive to end the stigma around mental health, foster open conversations, and fight the feeling of being a “square peg in a round hole.”
Profits from the festival will be donated to the Mental Health Coalition in support of its mission.
