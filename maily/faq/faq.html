Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions Answered

Where and when will the festival be held?

May 27th-29th in San Francisco at Hellmans Hallow in Golden Gate Park.

How much are tickets?

Tickets are $85 dollars for the three day festival.

Do you have to buy a ticket each day?

A single ticket will be issued and is valid for all three days so we encourage you to come all three days!

How will I receive my ticket?

A ticket will be emailed to you and on the first day you arrive you will receive a wristband at the gate. Keep the wristband on throughout the weekend to enter the festival each day.

What are the COVID-19 policies at the Speak Your Mind festival?

COVID-19 is a highly contagious virus that can lead to severe illness and death. We understand that at gatherings the virus is more inclined to spread. Thus, we will keep an eye on the cases and send updates as the event nears. As of now, masks are not required but they are encouraged.

Will there be food and drinks at the festival?

Yes! We will have local food trucks and vendors with a wonderful array of delicious cuisines!

Is the festival all ages?

We welcome all! Keep in mind of any triggering content.
